PROFILE

Role:Jr Electrical Engineer | Testing & Commissioning | MCore Technologies

July,2025 - Present

As a Junior Electrical Engineer specializing in Testing and Commissioning, I have been actively involved in the execution and validation of LV and MV electrical systems, including switchgear, transformers, protection relays, and control panels. My role includes performing critical tests such as insulation resistance, CT/PT ratio and polarity checks, breaker timing analysis, and functional relay logic verification to ensure system integrity and operational safety. I collaborate closely with senior engineers, consultants, and site managers to maintain strict compliance with industry standards, safety protocols, and project specifications. Additionally, I am responsible for preparing detailed test reports and documentation in accordance with client and regulatory requirements. My experience also includes coordination with OEM vendors during Factory Acceptance Tests (FAT) and Site Acceptance Tests (SAT), as well as troubleshooting and resolving issues that arise during commissioning activities. With hands-on knowledge of testing instruments like Omicron and Megger, and a working understanding of IEC and IS standards, I contribute to successful project handovers with a focus on reliability, safety, and performance. As part of the electrical meter installation and networking team, I am responsible for the physical installation, configuration, and network integration of smart meters. My work involves ensuring seamless communication between the meters and the central network, as well as troubleshooting connectivity issues.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Installation and testing of electrical meters, ensuring compliance with safety standards and regulations.
  • Configuration of communication modules for networking, including PLC (Power-Line Communication) and other wireless protocols.
  • Troubleshooting network connectivity issues and optimizing data transmission for real-time monitoring.
  • Collaborating with team members to ensure efficient and timely project completion.
  • Assist in troubleshooting electrical issues and support corrective measures during pre-commissioning and commissioning stages.
  • Conduct testing and commissioning of LV and MV electrical systems including switchgear, transformers, circuit breakers, relays, and control panels.
  • Collaborate with project engineers, consultants, and contractors to meet project deadlines and ensure smooth handover.
